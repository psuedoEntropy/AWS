- A region is is a physical location in the world which consists of two or more Availability Zone (AZs).
- An AZ is one or more discrete data centers with redundant power and networking & connectivity, housed in seperate facilities.
- Edge locations are endpoints for AWS which are used for caching conent. Typically this consists of CloudFront, Amazon's CDN
